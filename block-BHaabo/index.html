Why are we doing Altcampus?

AltCampus is an alternative to college that you wish existed. Currently, we are offering an in-house, 6-month long program to train people in quality software declopment, where they don't have to pay anything until they are hired.
We live in the world of digital skills. These skills are always evolving and so is their demand. Students/professionals who are not responsive to the changes in industry demands, in terms os skills, will have a hard time surviviing, well at least flourishing.
